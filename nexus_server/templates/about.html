The Phaze Story

We're on a mission to bring back the era of sovereign communication.

Why Phaze?

In 2014, Skype was the gold standard for staying in touch. It was fast, reliable, and familiar. But as the world moved toward centralized, ad-driven platforms, that classic experience was lost. We missed the bit-perfect Aero UI, the iconic sounds, and most importantly, the sense of privacy that came with peer-to-peer communication.

Our Mission

To provide a sovereign, production-grade forensic reconstruction of the classic communication experience, hardened with modern encryption and built for a post-centralized world.

Sovereign & Open

Phaze isn't just a remake; it's an engine built from scratch in Go. It uses end-to-end encryption (NaCl/Box) by default and features a three-layer mesh network that ensures you can stay connected even when central servers are unreachable.

We believe your communication belongs to you. No telemetry, no cloud dependencies, and zero-knowledge relays. That's the Phaze promise.

Join the Mesh

Phaze is built by sovereign engineers for sovereign users. Whether you're a forensic developer or just someone who misses the old days, there's a place for you in our network.
