Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-19
Short version: Your messages and calls are end-to-end encrypted. We can't read them. We run a relay, not a surveillance network.

1. What we collect

When you register an account, we store:

  • Your username, a bcrypt hash of your password, and your email address (used for verification and password reset).
  • Optionally, your display name, mood text, and phone number if you link one.
  • Your friends list and block list so we can route messages.
  • Connection metadata (IP address) transiently, for rate-limiting. We do not persist IP logs.

2. What we do not collect

  • The contents of your messages, calls, or file transfers — they are end-to-end encrypted with NaCl SecretBox (XSalsa20 + Poly1305) before they leave your device. The Phaze Nexus only sees encrypted blobs.
  • Your contacts' phone numbers, address-book, or device identifiers.
  • Browsing history, telemetry, or analytics. There are no third-party trackers in Phaze.

3. Third parties

  • Email delivery: verification and password-reset emails are delivered via an SMTP provider. The provider sees your email address and the subject line.
  • SMS delivery (if you opt in to phone verification): handled via Twilio. Twilio sees your phone number and the OTP.
  • Hosting: phazechat.world runs on a commercial hosting provider. Your IP is visible to the provider for as long as your connection is open.

4. Encryption

Every pairwise conversation is encrypted with keys generated on-device using x/crypto/nacl/box (Curve25519). Keys are pinned on first contact (Trust On First Use). We do not hold, escrow, or have any access to your private keys.

5. Your rights

  • Delete your account: email privacy@phazechat.world and we will purge your row from our database.
  • Export: your local SQLite database is yours. Back it up anywhere.
  • Change email/password: from the app's Settings panel.

6. Law enforcement requests

We publish our practice transparently: we will respond to lawful process in the jurisdiction of our hosting provider. Because messages are E2EE, we cannot hand over content we do not have. We can hand over the metadata in Section 1.

7. Changes

If we change this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date and note the change. Material changes will be announced to all verified email addresses at least 7 days before taking effect.

8. Contact

Questions or complaints: privacy@phazechat.world.
